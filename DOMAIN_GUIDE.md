## üîó Connecting Domain to Render.com

Once you have your domain:

1. **In Render.com Dashboard**:
   - Go to your service
   - Click "Settings" ‚Üí "Custom Domain"
   - Click "Add Custom Domain"
   - Enter your domain (e.g., `retroflix.app`)

2. **In Porkbun (or your registrar)**:
   - Login to domain dashboard
   - Go to DNS settings
   - Add these records:
     ```
     Type: CNAME
     Host: www
     Answer: retroflix.onrender.com
     TTL: 600

     Type: A
     Host: @
     Answer: 216.24.57.1
     TTL: 600
     ```

3. **Wait for DNS Propagation**:
   - Takes 10-60 minutes
   - Check status: https://dnschecker.org/

4. **SSL Certificate** (Auto-enabled):
   - Render automatically issues free SSL
   - Your site will be HTTPS

## üí° Pro Tips

### Saving Money:
1. **First-year discounts**: Many registrars offer 50% off first year
2. **Promo codes**: Search "[registrar name] coupon code" before buying
3. **Buy multi-year**: Often cheaper per year if you buy 2-3 years upfront
4. **Avoid upsells**: Decline email hosting, website builders, etc.

### Choosing the Right Domain:
1. **.com is king**: If available, always choose .com first
2. **.app is good**: Modern, professional, mobile-friendly association
3. **Avoid hyphens**: "retro-flix.com" is harder to remember
4. **Keep it short**: Easier to type and remember
5. **Check trademarks**: Make sure you're not infringing on existing brands

### Domain Security:
1. **Enable WHOIS privacy**: Keeps your personal info hidden (usually free)
2. **Enable 2FA**: Protect your domain account
3. **Lock domain**: Prevents unauthorized transfers
4. **Use strong password**: Unique password for domain registrar
